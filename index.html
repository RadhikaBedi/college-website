    How to Apply at Greenwood University

    Welcome! Here's a step-by-step guide to our application process.

    Application Deadlines

    • Fall 2025: July 31st, 2025
    • Spring 2026: December 15th, 2025
    • *Early application is strongly recommended.

    Required Documents

    • Completed Application Form
    • Official Academic Transcripts (High School / Previous College)
    • 10th and 12th Marksheetsy
    • Letters of Recommendation (2-3 recommended)
    • Government-issued ID (Aadhar, Voter ID, etc.)
    • Character Certificate

    Application Steps

    1. Create an account on our Online Application Portal.
    2. Fill out the application form with accurate details.
    3. Upload all required documents as specified.
    4. Submit your application and pay the application fee.
    5. Track your application status through the portal.

    About Greenwood University


    Founded in 1895, Greenwood University has grown from a small liberal arts college to a comprehensive research university with global recognition. Our 200-acre campus blends historic architecture with cutting-edge facilities.

    We take pride in our interdisciplinary approach to education, connecting students with real-world challenges and opportunities through our network of industry partnerships and community engagement initiatives.

    Mission

    To educate, inspire, and empower the next generation of leaders.

    vision

    A globally recognized center for innovation and excellence.
